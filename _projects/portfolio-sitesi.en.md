## About the Project

The site you're looking at. I designed it as a CV to introduce myself, a
showcase for the projects I've built, and a blog where I share my writing.

## Why Jekyll?

Thanks to GitHub Pages' built-in support, I can update the site simply by adding
markdown files to the `_posts/` and `_projects/` folders — no build step to fuss
over.

## Design

I went with a minimal, typography-focused approach — content always comes first.
Dark and light themes switch automatically based on the system preference.

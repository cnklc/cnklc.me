## About the Project

Rentificial grew out of a car-rental ERP into a web-based, industry-agnostic
rental management platform (SaaS). From a hotel room to a power drill, from a
yacht to a ski set, it manages inventory, availability, reservations and rental
tracking for any rentable asset in a single system.

## Highlights

- Industry-agnostic, multi-purpose rental management
- Inventory, availability and reservation tracking
- End-to-end digitization of rental processes

## Tech Stack

Built at Komut Teknolojisi with **C#**, **.NET** and **ASP.NET MVC**.

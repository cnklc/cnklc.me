## About the Project

BooqApp is the marketplace/storefront product of the Rentificial ecosystem. It
works as a two-sided rental platform: businesses using Rentificial could list
their products and collect rental requests, while individuals could rent out
items they weren't using (e.g. a PlayStation, a cordless drill).

## Highlights

- Listing and rental-request management for businesses
- C2C (consumer-to-consumer) rental experience for individuals
- End-to-end development of listing management and the request flow

## Tech Stack

Built at Komut Teknolojisi with **C#** and **.NET**.

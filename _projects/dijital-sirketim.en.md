## About the Project

Dijital Şirketim ("My Digital Company") is online software that lets small and
mid-sized companies manage their bookkeeping over the web. It simplifies core
accounting tasks — invoicing, income/expense tracking and current-account
management — into a single dashboard.

## Highlights

- Invoicing and invoice tracking
- Income/expense and current-account management
- Simplified accounting experience for small businesses

## Tech Stack

Built at Komut Teknolojisi with **C#** and **.NET**.

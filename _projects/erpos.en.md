## About the Project

Erpos is the point-of-sale (POS) application used across Colin's and Loft stores.
Sitting at the heart of store operations, it manages sales, register and payment
flows. A companion mobile app connected to it is also under active development.

## Highlights

- Manages in-store sales and register operations
- Mobile app connected to the POS system
- Reliable architecture suited to high transaction volume

## Tech Stack

Built at Colin's with **C#**, **.NET Core**, **Entity Framework** and
**PostgreSQL**.
